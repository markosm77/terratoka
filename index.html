Terratoka App — Digital Literacy & Rural Experiences

An all‑in‑one platform that introduces rural communities — especially kids — to computer literacy and cybersecurity basics, while connecting families to host unique rural experiences.

Digital Basics

Offline‑first lessons on safe browsing, passwords, privacy, and device care — built for low‑connectivity schools.

Cyber Awareness

Playful challenges led by mentors and diaspora volunteers to learn how to protect themselves online.

Host Experiences

“Airbnb‑style” listings for rural families: cooking classes, gardening, craft days — with fair, transparent payouts.

About Terratoka

Terratoka is an independent, community‑rooted initiative based in Albania. We’re in an early stage and growing with honesty and care. We’ve exchanged a few emails with the Municipality of Shkodra and explored an application with Active Mobility Albania — nothing formal yet.

Our belief is simple: everyone who joins the community is a partner. Ana, Kristo, and Henri — the core of Terratoka Youth — are our first partners. Together, we’re building a bridge between youth energy, culture, and eco‑action.
